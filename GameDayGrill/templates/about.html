We Are Game Day Grill

Welcome to Game Day Grill, where we've been crafting winning moments since 1965. Born from a shared love of food and sports, we've evolved into a beloved local spot, blending mouthwatering grills, signature classics, and a lively atmosphere.

Founded by friends with a vision, Game Day Grill is more than a restaurant – it's a community hub where laughter, victories, and friendships flourish. Our commitment to quality ingredients and a friendly atmosphere defines the Game Day experience.
Step into our vibrant space adorned with sports memorabilia, and indulge in a menu of grill masterpieces and game day classics. Every visit is a festive occasion, celebrating the joy of sports and delicious dishes.
As proud community members, we actively support local initiatives and events. Game Day Grill is more than a place to eat; it's a place where we give back and connect with our neighbors.
Whether you're a sports enthusiast or just seeking a welcoming spot, Game Day Grill invites you to be part of our story. Experience the difference – where every meal is a victory and every visit is a celebration. Cheers to winning moments at Game Day Grill!
We are part of the Wayzata High School Chapter in Plymouth, MN. Our names are Devang Sharma, Nitish Shirodkar, Siddharth Salapaka, and Aditya Chickmath.
